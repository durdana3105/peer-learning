# Code of Conduct



## Our Pledge

We are committed to creating a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone participating in this project.



## Expected Behavior

* Be respectful and supportive
* Use inclusive and professional language
* Accept constructive feedback gracefully
* Collaborate positively with others



## Unacceptable Behavior

* Harassment or discrimination
* Offensive or abusive language
* Personal attacks or trolling
* Sharing private information without permission



## Reporting Process

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it promptly.  



## How to report

\- Contact maintainers at discord.

\- Provide as much detail as possible, including the incident, date, individuals involved, and any relevant links/screenshots.



All reports will be handled \*\*confidentially\*\*. You can expect an acknowledgment of your report within \[e.g., 48 hours], and the maintainers will investigate fairly and promptly.

## 

## Enforcement and Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and maintaining a safe environment for all contributors.



## Enforcement Process 

\- Maintainers will review every reported incident.

\- They will investigate concerns fairly and impartially.

\- Appropriate actions will be determined based on the severity of the violation. These may include:

&#x20; - A private warning

&#x20; - Temporary restrictions (e.g., temporary ban from issues/PRs)

&#x20; - Permanent removal from the project/community



Decisions will be communicated clearly to the parties involved.



## Anti-Retaliation Statement 

Retaliation against anyone who reports a violation or participates in the investigation process will not be tolerated. Any retaliation will be treated as a serious violation of this Code of Conduct.



## Final Note

By participating in this project, you agree to follow this Code of Conduct and help maintain a positive community.
