In backend/controllers/cronController.js inside sendMentorshipCheckinReminders, the upsert to the notifications table uses onConflict: 'user_id,entity_id,type' with ignoreDuplicates: true. When a milestone is overdue, the cron job tries to send a new notification with the title 'Milestone Overdue'. However, because it shares the same user_id,entity_id,type as the 'Milestone Due Soon' notification previously sent, the upsert ignores it and the overdue notification is never created.
